Key definitions

A glossary of key terms which may be useful when playing music or discussing it with others. Knowledge of these terms may be assumed in other places on this site.

Term Definition
Semitone The distance from any key to the next key from it (no keys inbetween)
Whole tone A distance of 2 semitones (1 key inbetween)
Flat (â™­) The note 1 semitone to the left
Sharp (#) The note 1 semitone to the right
Chord 3 or more keys being pressed at once
Root note The note on which a chord is based, e.g a C major chord has a root note of C

Intervals

An interval is the distance between 2 notes. There are 12 intervals and each has a distinct sound. An interval can be expressed by the number of semitones between the 2 notes being played.
