Scales

The easiest way to explain scales is as a collection of notes that because of a musical reason have been grouped together. They are very useful especially if you want to improvise or write your own music.

Major Scales

To form a major scale, pick a root note to build the scale on. The major scale of that root note is then formed by

Whole tone - Whole tone - Semitone - Whole tone - Whole tone - Whole tone - Semitone
