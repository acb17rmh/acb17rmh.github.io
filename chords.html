Types of Chords

Chords are 3 or more notes being played at the same time. There are many different types of chords, this table shows some commonly used ones and what intervals they are made of.

Chord Intervals that chord is made from
Major 1-3-5
Minor 1-m3-5
Seven 1-3-5-m7
sus2 1-2-5
sus4 1-4-5
Diminished 1-m3-m5

Diatonic Major Chords

Diatonic chords are chords that occur naturally within a key. What this means is that the notes within these chords are all in their respective scale/key. The primary chords of any key are the I, IV and V diatonic chords. Note that the seventh variant of the V chord is also often used in its place.
